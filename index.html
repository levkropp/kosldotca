About KOSL

KOSL (Kropp Olsha Semiconductor Lithography) is developing open source processes and equipment for home semiconductor fabrication.

We believe the tools to make chips should be as accessible as the tools to make PCBs. Our work eliminates the need for toxic chemicals, cleanroom environments, and million-dollar equipment by replacing them with grocery store materials, desktop CNC machines, and open source software.

Every process recipe, equipment design, and experiment result is published openly. No patents. No paywalls.

Open Source

All designs, processes, and research published under permissive licenses.

Accessible

No specialty chemicals. No used equipment. Everything from Amazon and the grocery store.

Safe

No HF. No organic solvents. Water-based development. Grocery store etchants.
